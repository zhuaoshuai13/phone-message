Timeline
FLASH was created on Venus with the unlimited power of charging multiple smartphones in a flash. FLASH loves charging up smartphones whenever he gets the chance and often appears in random locations around the world when someone’s phone is running low. Charged with the lead of the Infinix exploration team. He explores Earth far and wide on his mission to provide a 180W mobile power station for anyone who needs it. MIRROR was created on Jupiter with a massive immersive screen that displays splendid scenes of his home planet. MIRROR travels the globe with the task of brightening people’s day with beautiful and ethereal landscape views of Jupiter to inspire their creativity to travel and create artwork of their own. Equipped with a massive immersive screen, MIRROR enjoys popping up in front of people lacking motivation and shows them mesmerizing scenes of Jupiter and the stars to boost their mood and add some color to their day. WISESTAR was created on Mars with a unique processor capable of speeds never experienced before on Earth. Tasked with the mission of eliminating lag and loading times, WISESTAR travels the globe in search of people having a hard time installing software, rendering videos, editing photos, or playing their favorite games. He swoops in and boosts their processing speed into the stars giving them instantaneous loading times. Equipped with a ZERO ULTRA All-Around 6nm 5G processor, WISESTAR solves daily problems and absolutely loves removing lag from people’s lives worldwide. VISION was created on Mercury with a next-generation telescopic camera lens that captures exciting moments from many miles away. With the task of other-worldly photography, VISON travels the globe snapping pictures for people in moments he thinks they would want to remember for a lifetime. Equipped with a ZERO ULTRA 200MP OIS resolution camera, he takes photography to new heights and bounds, inspiring Infinix fans worldwide to up their game and take stunning photos of the beautiful planet they live on. CHIC was created on Saturn with an other-worldly style that instantaneously appeals to everyone he meets. Tasked with making Infinix fans worldwide more stylish and chic, he travels the globe giving people stylish and fashionable makeovers to make them look their best selves. Equipped with the magical and mysterious looks of the Infinix ZERO ULTRA, CHIC uses his power to instantly make everyone he meets look fantastic.
